Private World Model for Enterprise Operations

Built on AI + OR research from the University of Hong Kong ecosystem, OptiMax AI is developing a secure, controllable, and efficient private world model for enterprise operations, making business work smarter, faster, and simpler.

OptiMax AI Limited is a cutting-edge artificial intelligence company rooted in Hong Kong. We are dedicated to developing secure, controllable, and efficient private world models for enterprise operations. Founded in Hong Kong in September 2025, the company is a key startup supported by the Hong Kong Innovation and Technology Commission's RAISe+ program.

The company is led by Professor Zuo-Jun Max Shen, a Foreign Member of the Chinese Academy of Engineering, former Vice-President (Research) of the University of Hong Kong, and a globally renowned scholar in operations research and supply chain management. The team integrates frontier research in AI, operations research, and enterprise operations, translating academic breakthroughs into forecasting, planning, scheduling, risk intelligence, and intelligent Q&A for real business scenarios.

OptiMax AI has advanced cooperation around enterprise scenarios with companies including TCL and China Mobile, covering operational risk management, intelligent procurement, production scheduling, master planning, inventory optimization, and logistics optimization. The company has completed its angel round financing and aims to become a global innovation force in intelligent enterprise operations.

OptiMax Enterprise AI is under active development and is expected to reach the market in September 2026.

FOUNDING CHAIR

Prof. Zuo-Jun Max Shen

BEng, ME, MS, PhD, FINFORMS, FPOMS, FHKEng

Professor Shen is a Foreign Member of the Chinese Academy of Engineering, served as Vice-President (Research) of the University of Hong Kong, and is currently a chaired professor jointly appointed by the Faculty of Engineering and the Faculty of Business and Economics at the University of Hong Kong. He concurrently serves as the Dean of the Graduate School, Acting Director of the Technology Transfer Office, and Acting Director of the Knowledge Exchange Office. He is the Founding Director of the Jasper Lo Chit Chong Data Science Institute.

Professor Shen received his PhD from Northwestern University in 2000. He joined the University of Florida as an Assistant Professor the same year, and joined UC Berkeley in 2004, where he was later promoted to Chancellor's Professor and Chair of the Department of Industrial Engineering and Operations Research, as well as Professor of Civil and Environmental Engineering. He joined HKU in 2021.

Professor Shen's primary research areas include logistics and supply chain management, data-driven decision-making, and system optimization. He is a Fellow of INFORMS, a Fellow and former President of POMS, and a Fellow of the Hong Kong Academy of Engineering Sciences.
