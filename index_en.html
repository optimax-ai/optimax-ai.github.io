About Us

A Secure, Reliable, and Practical Private Supply Chain AI Decision Brain for Enterprises

OptiMax AI Limited is a cutting-edge artificial intelligence company rooted in Hong Kong. We are dedicated to developing secure, controllable, and efficient enterprise-level private supply chain large language models, aspiring to become the "AI Decision Brain" that drives intelligent business operations. Founded in September 2025 in Hong Kong, our team is supported by the Hong Kong Innovation and Technology Commission's RAISe+ program as a key startup. With AI empowerment and data-driven strategies at our core, we help global enterprises achieve profound digital transformation and decision-making upgrades.

The company is led by Professor Zuo-Jun Max Shen, Vice-President (Research) of the University of Hong Kong and a globally renowned scholar in operations research and supply chain management. Under his leadership, we have assembled a core team of exceptional young scientists and engineers from top universities worldwide, deeply integrating cutting-edge research in AI, operations research, and supply chain management. Leveraging HKU's first-class research and innovation platform, OptiMax AI translates the latest academic breakthroughs into innovative solutions that address real-world business challenges.

OptiMax AI has already established cooperation intentions with several well-known enterprises, including TCL and China Mobile, to build the next-generation enterprise AI engine. The company has completed its angel round financing and is experiencing strong growth momentum, aiming to become a global innovation force in the AI decision-making space.

OptiMax Enterprise AI is currently under active development and is expected to launch in September 2026.

Prof. Max Shen

Prof. Zuo-Jun Max Shen

Prof. Zuo-Jun Max Shen

BEng, ME, MS, PhD, FINFORMS, FPOMS, FHKEng

Professor Shen is the Vice-President (Research) of the University of Hong Kong, a chaired professor jointly appointed by the Faculty of Engineering and the Faculty of Business and Economics, and concurrently serves as the Dean of the Graduate School, Acting Director of the Technology Transfer Office, and Acting Director of the Knowledge Exchange Office. He is the Founding Director of the The Jasper Lo Chit Chong Data Science Institute.

Professor Shen received his PhD from Northwestern University in 2000. He joined the University of Florida as an Assistant Professor the same year, and joined UC Berkeley in 2004, where he was later promoted to Chancellor's Professor and Chair of the Department of Industrial Engineering and Operations Research, as well as Professor of Civil and Environmental Engineering. He joined HKU in 2021.

Professor Shen's primary research areas include logistics and supply chain management, data-driven decision-making, and system optimization. He is a Fellow of INFORMS, a Fellow and former President of POMS, and a Fellow of the Hong Kong Academy of Engineering Sciences.
