## Design Context

### Users
OptiMax AI homepage visitors include enterprise decision makers, potential partners, investors, researchers, and candidates. They need to quickly understand the company's credibility, product direction, team strength, hiring needs, and contact paths.

### Brand Personality
The brand should feel premium, trustworthy, practical, and platform-grade. It should communicate enterprise AI product capability without looking like a generic large-model demo site.

### Aesthetic Direction
Use the DiDi Global homepage as the primary visual reference: clean full-width sections, calm typography, large hero imagery, restrained navigation, generous whitespace, and a polished platform-company feel. Preserve the current homepage content while removing the older Bootstrap template impression.

### Design Principles
1. Keep business content stable while improving hierarchy, spacing, and visual polish.
2. Prefer clean platform-brand composition over decorative AI effects.
3. Use restrained neutral surfaces with OptiMax blue as the primary brand accent, while keeping DiDi's clean platform-company structure as the design reference.
4. Make founders, hiring, and contact sections feel integrated into one company story.
5. Keep the static site lightweight and compatible with the existing Bootstrap/Worthy stack.
